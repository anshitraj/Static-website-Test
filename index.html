About Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced sport played between two teams. It was invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith. The goal is to shoot the basketball through the opponent's hoop and we have to defend the opponent so that they cant shoot the basketball in our hoop

Rules of Basketball

  • Each team has 5 players on the court.
  • The game consists of four quarters, each lasting 10-12 minutes.
  • Players must dribble the ball while moving.
  • No physical contact is allowed between players.
  • Points are scored by shooting the ball through the hoop.
